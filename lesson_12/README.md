# Lesson 12: Reading Group II

## Overview
Second reading group session to deepen coverage or explore emerging alignment methods. Use this to follow up on open questions from Lesson 11.

## Learning objectives
- Synthesize prior readings with new developments.
- Critically evaluate empirical claims and methodology.
- Propose next steps for experiments or further study.

## Lesson structure
1. Paper selection with emphasis on gaps
   - Complement Lesson 11 themes
2. Lightning reviews
   - 2 to 3 minute summaries per paper
3. Technical deep dive
   - Formalism, objective choices, and evaluation
4. Comparison session
   - How methods relate to PPO/GRPO/GSPO/SAPO
5. Wrap-up
   - Updated reading list and open problems

## Suggested prep
- Prepare a comparison table or slide per paper.
