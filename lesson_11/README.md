# Lesson 11: Reading Group I

## Overview
This session is a flexible reading group focused on a small set of papers or technical reports that support the course goals. The exact topics can be chosen closer to the date.

## Learning objectives
- Practice reading and presenting technical papers.
- Connect new results to course foundations.
- Identify open questions and research directions.

## Lesson structure
1. Topic selection and paper assignment
   - Choose 2 to 4 papers with a shared theme
2. Pre-read guidance
   - Key questions and definitions to track
3. Short paper summaries (per presenter)
   - Problem, method, main results, limitations
4. Deep dive discussion
   - Derivations, assumptions, and failure modes
5. Synthesis
   - Common themes and takeaways
6. Action items
   - Follow-up experiments or reading list updates

## Suggested prep
- Read assigned papers and prepare a 5 to 10 minute summary.
